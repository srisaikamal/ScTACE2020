UCD Student Accommodation

Rooms on-campus can be booked via the Online Registration System (link will be updated later). Every guest sharing summer student housing at UCD enjoys their own bedroom, giving them maximum comfort and privacy. UCD's residential accommodation comprises of comfortable 4-bedroom and 6-bedroom suites – with free WiFi throughout. The apartments are positioned throughout the college campus and at nearby Blackrock, a lively, friendly village on Dublin’s affluent Southside, less than 2 miles from Belfield. Breakfast is not included in price but guests can eat at the main restaurant or nearby shops.

The Radisson Blu, St. Helen’s Hotel - 5

The hotel features a four-storey wing of Portland stone with 151 elegant rooms and 25 exclusive Business Class Suites.Panoramic views of the gardens, Dublin Bay and Howth Head complement the recently upgraded furnishings. All hotel rooms in this Dublin estate have an LCD cable television, luxurious Egyptian cotton sheets, duck down duvets and free high-speed, wireless Internet access. Smoking and non-smoking rooms are available, as well as wheelchair accessible rooms. The hotel is the closest hotel to the conference venue, located just across the road from the UCD campus and approximately a 15 - 20 mins walk to the conference venue.

Clayton Hotel Burlington Road - 4

Just 30 minutes from Dublin International Airport and a 15-minute stroll to St. Stephen’s Green, Clayton Hotel Burlington Road is the ideal base for your stay in Dublin. Offering comfort and space, ideal for business trips and leisure breaks to Dublin. Our Lobby Lounge is the perfect place to meet. We serve a delicious range of coffees, teas, fresh pastries, and snacks. Unwind with a craft beer or a glass of wine and sample our extensive bar food menu. Proximity to UCD: 18-minute bus / 42-minute walk
To take advantage of the discounted rates*, delegates should book via the hotel website and enter the promo-code ICRS180721.
*Limited rooms available. First come first served.

Clayton Hotel Ballsbridge - 4

Clayton Hotel Ballsbridge is one of the finest 4-star hotels in the heart of Dublin 4. It is located only a 3-minute walk to the RDS & in the heart of the business and embassy district, serviced by many bus routes and the DART, and is within walking distance of Dublin city centre and the Aviva Stadium. This charming Victorian built hotel has 335 bedrooms, an underground car park, and the Grandstand Bar and Restaurant. Proximity to UCD: 26-minute bus / 34-minute walk.
To take advantage of the discounted rates*, delegates should book via the hotel website and enter the promo-code ICRS072021.
*Limited rooms available. First come first served.

The Talbot Hotel Stillorgan - 4

The Stillorgan Park Hotel is one of Dublin’s premier Failte Ireland and Automobile Association (AA) rated hotels. The conference venue, UCD, is located on the Stillorgan Road (N11) approximately 5 -10 minutes by bus or car from the Stillorgan Park Hotel. Rooms include comfortable bedding, en-suites bathrooms, air conditioning, complimentary Wi-Fi, work desk area, tea & coffee making facilities.

The Ballsbridge Hotel - 4

Famous for its traditional Irish hospitality and quality service, Ballsbridge Hotel is one of the most renowned, stylish and luxurious hotels in Dublin’s south. Ballsbridge Hotel is ideally located just 20 minutes from the city centre in leafy Dublin 4, making it the perfect base for both business and leisure travel. For dining, there’s plenty of choice, and there’s always craic to be had in The Dubliner Pub – particularly on a match day! The Dubliner Pub is where Ballsbridge locals and hotel residents alike meet to enjoy excellent food, drinks, and warm atmosphere.
