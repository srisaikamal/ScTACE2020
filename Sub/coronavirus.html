COVID-19 (CORONAVIRUS): ICRS 2020 IS NOW POSTPONED TO 20-22 JULY 2021


Dear icRS 2020 Conference Submitter,

If you have an accepted contribution and were due to present in the postponed icRS 2020, please sit back and relax as there is no action required from you at this time. Your submission(s) and registration will automatically be transferred to the new conference dates.
If you should wish to update or amend your abstract or paper, we will be pleased to accommodate you provided we receive this from you by February 1st 2021. We also intend to reopen the submission for abstracts and papers in case additional delegates might wish to submit: the deadline for this will be December 1st 2020.

Please note that new Submission Acceptance Letters and Visa Invitation Letters will be issued in early 2021 for those registered delegates requiring one for visa and/or institutional purposes.
Please don’t hesitate to contact events@conferenceorganisers.ie if you have any questions.

The icRS Committee
