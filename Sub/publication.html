The presented and selected conference proceedings (full conference articles) will be invited to submit an extended version for publication in a special issue of the Springer Book Series, Sustainable Production, Life Cycle Engineering and Management (ISSN: 2194-0541). Furthermore, conference proceedings will be also published by IOP conference series: Materials Science and Engineering and consequently indexed in Conference Proceedings Citation Index-Science (CPCI-S) (Thomson Reuters, Web of Science), Compendex, Inspec, and Scopus. Each article will be allocated a Digital Object Identifier (DOI) address.

The series of International Conferences on Resource Sustainability (icRS) are sponsored by Resources, Conservation & Recycling journal. High-quality presentations (either extended abstract or full conference paper) from the icRS 2021 will be recommended to various journals that support the conference (see below), including a dedicated Virtual Special Issue (VSI) that would be organized by Resources, Conservation & Recycling (2017 Impact Factor: 7.044). The following journals (see logos below) will support icRS 2021 by inviting selected conference presentations to submit an extended article for possible consideration in various special issues. Editors from supporting journals will evaluate both oral and poster presentations during the conference and invite selected presentations to submit their full extended papers for a special issue.
