Registration includes:
Delegate conference pack
Lunches
Tea & coffee breaks
Conference welcome reception

A single registration fee is required for each accepted conference paper, extended abstract or poster presentation. At least one author must register, pay the appropriate registration fee and present the contribution (conference article, extended abstract or poster) at the conference in order to ensure the inclusion of an accepted contribution in the final conference program. Additional presentations (up to two extra presentations) by the same registering author are subject to additional fees (140 Euros per each extra contribution) rather than a full regular registration fee.

Participants requiring visas should register as early as possible after receiving the acceptance decision letter in order to allow for enough time for the visa application process. A formal invitation letter will be sent upon request to participants after completion of the registration process.

For queries or assistance, please contact the registration desk at:
Email: events@conferenceorganisers.ie

Student registration
Registered students will have access to all conference sessions, industrial exhibitions, workshops, conference delegate materials, reception(s), lunches and coffee breaks. The conference banquet is not included in this registration fee. A letter confirming current student status (dated within two weeks before fee payment) and a valid student card will need to be uploaded to the conference payment webpage to confirm eligibility for the student registration fee.

Full delegate registration
The full-delegate registration fee guarantees access to all conference sessions, industrial exhibitions, workshops, conference delegate materials, reception(s), lunches and coffee breaks. The conference banquet is not included in the registration fee. Tickets for this can be purchased separately via the conference payment webpage and included in the total conference registration fee.
