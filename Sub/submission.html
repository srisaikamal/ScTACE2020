>> Information for Authors who have already accepted contributions related to the postponed icRS2020

icRS 2021 will accept two types of submissions, i.e. full conference articles, and extended abstracts. Participation, as an oral or poster presenter, in the conference is possible by either of these two types of submissions. For instance, if you have decided to participate in the conference with submitting an extended abstract, then, there is no need to submit a full paper afterward. Editors from supporting journals will evaluate both oral and poster presentations during the conference and invite selected presentations to submit their full extended papers for a special issue.

Types of Submissions
Full conference articles: Authors are invited to submit papers (maximum 6-pages length including a title, abstract, keywords, main body, figure(s), table(s) and references) reporting original research of theoretical or applied nature. Accepted papers exceeding the normal length may be included in the proceedings upon payment of over-length page charges for each page in excess of six pages, up to a maximum of two additional pages. Two broad categories of full conference articles can be submitted to the conference:

Original research articles: These articles report original research with broad scientific significance and importance. Case study and empirical research articles fall into this category as well.

Review: These papers provide an extensive overview and thorough assessment of recent developments in selected areas that fall within the aims and scope of the conference.

Extended abstract: The extended abstract must be clear and concise addressing scientific questions that are relevant to the topic(s) of the conference. The abstracts should be no more than 500-600 words, with a clear description of the research question, importance, and relevance of the research question, methods, and results and main findings or expected results and findings. A maximum one figure/table can be included in the extended abstract submission.

Any queries regarding extended abstract or full conference article submission, please contact conference chair, Pezhman Ghadimi (pezhman.ghadimi@ucd.ie) or conference program chair, Chao Wang (chaowanghn@vip.163.com)
The submission deadline for extended abstracts and full conference papers is December 1st, 2020 .


Each poster must be in portrait format.
Each poster must be printed (at the cost of the author) and SHOULD NOT BE BIGGER than the following size:
Height/tall= 1189mm, Width=841mm (A0 Portrait).

Any poster printed in landscape or larger than the provided board for display, will not be displayed at the conference.
Each poster presenter will be provided with special Velcro stickers from the registration desk to attach their poster to their assigned board.
Posters printed on canvas or heavy material are not recommended as they often prove too heavy to attach to poster boards, pins may not be used on the poster boards provided, only Velcro strips are permitted.
