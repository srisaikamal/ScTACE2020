Paper Submission through PROCEDIA (Conference Managing System).

DETAILED PROCEDURE FOR SUBMISSION OF ABSTRACT THROUGH PROCEDIA (CONFERENCE MANAGING SYSTEM)

• Click on the link and it will be directed to the PROCEDIA official page with a registration form.

• The corresponding author needs to complete the registration form and sign up.

• Upon the aforesaid step, the corresponding author will receive a mail with the PROCEDIA Activation Link, which has to be clicked.

• Now, using the same PROCEDIA LINK, the corresponding author can sign in to their created account with their login credentials.

• The corresponding author will get to see an option called "ADD NEW MANUSCRIPT". And now, the title of the respective paper has to be given in the particulars and click on add new manuscript.

• Then the corresponding author will be directed to a new page where the authors details should be given and continue further by clicking on COMPLETE YOUR SUBMISSION

• Finally, it will direct to a page where the manuscript details should be given and upload the document of the paper.

• Finally, go for FINALIZE YOUR SUBMISSION.

DETAILED PROCEDURE FOR SUBMISSION OF FULL LENGTH PAPER THROUGH PROCEDIA (CONFERENCE MANAGING SYSTEM)

FOR AUTHORS WHO HAVE SUBMITTED ABSTRACT

• Login to your PROCEDIA account and under the category of MY MANUSCRIPT, the corresponding author can see their submitted abstract.

• Click on FULL PAPER SUBMISSION and upload the respective full length paper.

• Finally, go for FINALIZE YOUR SUBMISSION.

FOR AUTHORS WHO HAVENT SUBMITTED ABSTRACT

• The corresponding author has to repeat the entire procedure as stated for the abstract submission and in place of abstract, full length paper has to be submitted.

NOTE:The above mentioned detailing in the table is applicable for journals / proceedings other than SPRINGER.
